# wattie

EV charging intelligence.  
Clear charging data, without the guesswork.

Wattie is an analytical web app for EV owners.  
It imports charging data and turns it into what actually matters — real charging costs, price per km, charging losses, and battery behavior.

Not another dashboard.  
Not a charge finder.  
Not a live companion app.

Wattie helps you understand what really happens when you charge your car.  [oai_citation:0‡wattie_brand_guidelines.md](sediment://file_000000007e2c720a8fa53c2418c3b2d4)

## Focus

- charging session analysis
- real charging efficiency
- AC / DC charging loss visibility
- price per km
- monthly charging costs
- battery behavior insights
